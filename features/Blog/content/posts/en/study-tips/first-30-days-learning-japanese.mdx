## Frequently Asked Questions

### What if I fall behind?

Don't restart! Pick up where you left off. Progress isn't perfect.

### Is 30 minutes enough?

For beginners, yes! Quality and consistency matter more than quantity.

### What next after 30 days?

Continue with kanji basics, more grammar (past tense, negative), and vocabulary expansion!
