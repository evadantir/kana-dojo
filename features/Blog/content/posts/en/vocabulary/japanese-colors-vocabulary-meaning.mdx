## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why does é’ mean both blue AND green?

Historically, Japanese didn't distinguish blue and green as separate colors! One word covered the whole blue-green spectrum. ç·‘ (midori) became more common later, but old expressions kept é’.

### What color should I wear to a funeral?

Black (é»’) is the modern standard, though white (ç™½) is the traditional mourning color. Avoid bright colors like red.

### What's the luckiest color in Japan?
