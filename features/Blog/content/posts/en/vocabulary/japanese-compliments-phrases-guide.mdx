## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is it okay to compliment strangers in Japan?

It depends on context. Complimenting service staff on their work or someone's pet is generally fine. Personal appearance compliments to strangers might feel uncomfortable. When in doubt, stick to situational compliments.

### How often should I give compliments?

Less frequently than in Western cultures, but sincerely. A well-timed, genuine compliment means more than constant praise. Quality over quantity!

### What if someone deflects my compliment repeatedly?
